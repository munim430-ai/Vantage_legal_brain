## Copy Rules (apply to all routes)

- Second person throughout: "Your factory", "You will receive", "We deliver"
- Maximum 20 words per sentence in client-facing paragraphs
- Lead every section with the outcome, not the process
- Prices always in BDT with commas: BDT 55,000
- Use "gap scan" for VANTAGE's own assessment; never call it an "audit"
- Use "workers" not "employees" or "labour"
- Use "factory" not "plant", "facility", or "establishment"
- Approved terms only: compliance guidance, audit preparation, gap report, corrective action plan, remediation support
- Forbidden terms: certify, guarantee audit pass, legal advice, official audit, verification body, law firm, government approved, 100% compliant
