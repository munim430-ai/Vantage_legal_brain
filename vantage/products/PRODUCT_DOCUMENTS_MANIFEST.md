## Product Workflow Supported

### Free Gap Scan to Paid Sprint

1. Use `VANTAGE_Free_Gap_Scan_Form.docx` to collect initial findings.
2. Use `VANTAGE_1_Page_Gap_Report_Template.docx` to show the factory visible risk.
3. Use `BLA2026_Guide_VANTAGE-1.docx` as the supporting reference material.
4. Convert the factory into BLA 2026 Compliance Sprint using legal/commercial documents from `vantage/legal/`.
