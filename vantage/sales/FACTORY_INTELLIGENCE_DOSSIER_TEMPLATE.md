## Legal Positioning — Read Before Filling

- This dossier records publicly visible signals only. Do not record assumptions, rumours, or private audit data unless the factory shared it directly with you.
- Do not state or imply that a factory has failed an audit, violated the law, or has verified compliance gaps.
- Do not share this dossier with any third party.
- Outreach messages sent using this dossier must use legal-safe language at all times.
- If you discover a serious safety concern (possible child labour, imminent fire risk, active government investigation), stop commercial outreach and consult a licensed advocate before proceeding.
- Your outreach positions VANTAGE as offering compliance guidance and audit-preparation support — not audit services, legal advice, or certification.

## Section 4 — Legal-Safe Risk Interpretation

When interpreting signals for outreach purposes, apply these rules without exception.

### Approved language

| Instead of… | Say… |
|---|---|
| "Your factory failed an audit" | "Publicly visible signals suggest buyer-facing compliance pressure" |
| "You have compliance violations" | "There may be documentation gaps worth checking before a buyer visit" |
| "We found non-compliance" | "The gap scan may surface areas to strengthen" |
| "Your records are wrong" | "The gap scan can confirm your records are complete and audit-preparation ready" |
| "You will fail the audit" | "This is worth addressing before your next audit or buyer review" |
| "You are not compliant" | "The gap scan shows where to focus before a formal review" |
| "We checked your compliance status" | "Based on publicly available information, this may be relevant" |
| "Audit" (for VANTAGE service) | "Gap scan" or "audit-preparation review" |
| "Certified compliant" | "Audit-preparation ready" |
| "Legal advice" | Do not use — ever |

### Rules for using compliance signals in outreach

1. Never reference private audit data unless the factory shared it directly with you in writing.
2. Never name a specific gap as a confirmed finding before running the gap scan.
3. Use publicly visible signals only to justify why the outreach is relevant — not to make compliance claims.
4. If a factory asks "did you audit us?" — answer clearly: "No. VANTAGE has not audited your factory. This is an introductory contact based on publicly available information."
5. The gap scan — not the dossier — is what determines actual compliance risk.

| 7 | No buyer logo / no certification / no compliance page | **Free gap scan angle** — keep it simple, lead with the free 25-question review | Template 5D |
| 8 | Contact is a Compliance Manager or HR Manager | **Peer professional angle** — speak directly about documentation burden, not buyer pressure | Template 5F |
| 9 | Contact is MD or Director | **MD/owner angle** — speak to buyer relationship risk, not documentation tasks | Template 5G |
| 10 | Contact came via referral or mutual connection | **Soft referral angle** — open with the connection, then offer the scan | Template 5H |

**Angle selection logic:**

```
IF upcoming_audit_signal = YES                → prioritize angle 1 or 3, depending on signals
IF RSC_listed = YES                            → use angle 2
IF certification_claimed AND no_audit_framework_listed → use angle 3
IF no_compliance_page AND no_certification     → use angle 7
IF contact_role = Compliance Manager OR HR Manager → use angle 8
IF contact_role = MD OR Director               → use angle 9
IF source = referral                           → use angle 10
DEFAULT                                        → use angle 7
```

## Section 10 — Stop Conditions

Stop outreach to this factory immediately if any of the following conditions are met. Record the stop condition in the `factories` tab under `stop_condition` and set `stage` to `Closed Lost`.

| Condition | Stop condition code | What to say |
|---|---|---|
| Factory asks not to be contacted again | `OPT_OUT` | "Understood. I will not contact you again. Thank you for your time." |
| Factory demands a certification guarantee as a condition of engagement | `CERT_DEMAND` | "VANTAGE does not issue compliance certificates. That is not a service we offer." |
| Factory asks for a legal opinion or legal advice | `LEGAL_OPINION` | "VANTAGE provides compliance guidance, not legal services. For legal advice, please consult a licensed advocate." |
| Factory requests an official audit result from VANTAGE | `AUDIT_DEMAND` | "VANTAGE is not an audit body. Audits are conducted by BSCI, WRAP, Sedex, and similar third-party bodies." |
| Factory asks VANTAGE to misrepresent compliance status to a buyer | `MISREP_REQUEST` | "That is not something VANTAGE can do. We provide compliance guidance only — we cannot make representations to buyers on your behalf." |
| Factory asks VANTAGE to claim affiliation with BGMEA, BSCI, or any audit body | `FALSE_AFFIL` | "VANTAGE is an independent advisory service. We are not affiliated with any audit body or trade association." |
| Active government investigation (DIFE, Labour Court) suspected | `GOVT_INVEST` | Stop commercial outreach. Tell factory: "This situation requires a licensed advocate. VANTAGE cannot advise during an active investigation." |
| Possible child labour or imminent safety issue visible publicly | `SAFETY_RISK` | Stop commercial outreach entirely. Consult a licensed advocate before any further contact. |

## Appendix B — Forbidden Phrases Reference

Never use these phrases in any outreach message, WhatsApp, LinkedIn note, or verbal introduction.

| Forbidden | Why |
|---|---|
| "failed audit" | Accuses factory of a specific outcome VANTAGE did not verify |
| "violations found" | Implies a formal finding VANTAGE is not qualified to make |
| "non-compliant" | Compliance determination requires audit authority |
| "we checked your records" | Suggests unauthorized access or surveillance |
| "legal advice" | VANTAGE is not a law firm |
| "certified compliant" | VANTAGE cannot issue certifications |
| "you will pass" or "we guarantee" | Prohibited under LEGAL_POSITIONING_RULES.md §3.2 |
| "audit result" | VANTAGE does not produce audit results |
| "government required" | Unless directly citing a specific regulation |
| "we work with BGMEA / BSCI / Sedex" | VANTAGE is not affiliated with any of these bodies |
