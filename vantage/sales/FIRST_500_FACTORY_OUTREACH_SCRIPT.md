# VANTAGE — First 500 Factory Outreach Script

**Status:** Active — v1.0  
**Owner:** Keystone Consultancy trading as VANTAGE  
**For:** Munim — daily field use  
**Purpose:** Contact the first 500 Bangladesh RMG factories, push them to complete the Free Gap Scan at `https://vantage-legal-brain.vercel.app/gap-scan`, and convert Medium/High/Critical results into a BDT 55,000 BLA 2026 Compliance Sprint.

---

## Legal Positioning (Read Before Every Outreach Session)

- The Free Gap Scan is compliance guidance and audit-preparation review. It is not an audit.
- VANTAGE does not provide legal advice. Never use the words "legal advice," "legal opinion," or "certified."
- No audit result is guaranteed. Do not imply any factory will pass or fail any audit.
- Use only approved terms: **gap scan, compliance guidance, audit-preparation support, corrective action plan**.
- Forbidden terms: audit pass guarantee, certified compliant, legal advice, official audit, government approved, 100% compliant.

## Section 1 — Target Factory Profile

### Tier 1 — Highest Priority (contact first)

| Signal | Why |
|---|---|
| Upcoming BSCI, WRAP, SA8000, or Sedex/SMETA audit within 8 weeks | Urgency is real. Sprint timeline fits. |
| Recently issued a CAP by an audit body | Buyer pressure is active. Pain is confirmed. |
| Received a buyer compliance request or demand letter | Budget authority exists. Decision is already needed. |
| Factory in Dhaka, Gazipur, Ashulia, or Narayanganj | Practical service delivery without travel costs. |
| 100–5,000 workers | Large enough for international audit requirements. Small enough for one-person Sprint. |

### Tier 2 — Strong Prospects

| Signal | Why |
|---|---|
| Visible public buyer/export signal | Buyer-facing factories often need organized labour, wage, welfare, grievance, and safety documentation for audit-preparation reviews. |
| Member of BGMEA, BKMEA, or BEPZA | Contact directory is accessible. |
| No known compliance consultant on retainer | Clean opportunity. No incumbent to displace. |
| Has not had a recent public audit signal | Documentation may be worth reviewing before the next buyer or audit-preparation cycle. |

### Tier 3 — Background Pipeline

- Factories outside Dhaka/Gazipur (Sprint deliverable virtually — flag transport separately)
- Factories under 100 workers (BLA audit requirements apply but urgency may be lower)
- Factories that passed a recent audit (nurture with Intelligence Brief; trigger re-engagement at 12 months)

## Section 2 — Who to Contact

Contact in this priority order within a single factory:

| Role | Why | Channel |
|---|---|---|
| **Managing Director / Owner** | Signs the MSA and authorises payment | WhatsApp, Phone |
| **Director (Finance / Operations)** | Budget holder. Can authorise BDT 55,000 without a board meeting. | WhatsApp, Phone |
| **Compliance Manager** | Understands the problem immediately. Can champion internally. | WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Phone |
| **HR Manager** | Day-to-day gap scan contact. Runs the session. | WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Facebook |

**Rule:** Do not go lower than HR Manager. Factory admin and floor supervisors cannot authorise payment.

**Rule:** If you reach the HR Manager but not the MD, ask the HR Manager: *"Is your Managing Director or a Director available for 15 minutes? The gap scan results will need their attention."*

## Section 11 — Objection Handling

### "BDT 55,000 অনেক বেশি।" / "Too expensive."

> "বুঝতে পারছি। Sprint-এ যা পাচ্ছেন: Gap Report, Corrective Action Plan, সর্বোচ্চ ৫টি পলিসি ডকুমেন্ট — ৩–৫ কার্যদিবসে, Bangla ও English-এ। অডিটে Major CAP হলে বায়ার শিপমেন্ট হোল্ড করতে পারে — সেই ক্ষতি BDT 55,000-এর অনেক বেশি।
>
> পুরো Sprint এখন না করলে, আমরা নির্দিষ্ট ডকুমেন্ট দিয়ে শুরু করতে পারি — BDT 3,000–5,000 প্রতি ডকুমেন্ট। কিন্তু Sprint প্যাকেজই সবচেয়ে দ্রুত সমাধান।"

**Rule: Never reduce the Sprint below BDT 55,000.**

---

### "ভাবতে হবে।" / "Need time to think."

> "অবশ্যই। কিন্তু যাওয়ার আগে — একটা নির্দিষ্ট সময় ঠিক করতে পারি? [দিন], [সময়]-এ আবার কথা বলা সম্ভব? আপনার অডিট উইন্ডো [X সপ্তাহ] — Sprint শুরু না করলে [তারিখ]-এর আগে ডেলিভারি সম্ভব হবে না।"

**For High/Critical:** follow up in 48 hours maximum.  
**For Medium:** follow up within 5 working days.

---

### "আমাদের কেউ আছে।" / "We already have a consultant."

> "ভালো খবর। তারা কী কভার করছেন জানতে পারি? BLA 2026-এর নতুন সংশোধনী এবং 2023-এর মিনিমাম ওয়েজ রিভিশনে যে নির্দিষ্ট ডকুমেন্টেশন রিকোয়ারমেন্ট যোগ হয়েছে, অনেক পরামর্শদাতা এখনো আপডেট করেননি। আমি একটা দ্বিতীয় মতামত হিসেবে কাজ করতে পারি — কোনো চার্জ নেই প্রাথমিক গ্যাপ স্ক্যানে।"

---

### "অডিটের পরে দেখব।" / "We'll wait until after the audit."

> "এটা একটা বিকল্প। কিন্তু অডিটে Critical গ্যাপ পেলে বায়ার CAP ইস্যু করে — এবং কিছু ক্ষেত্রে শিপমেন্ট সাসপেন্ড করে। সেই পরিস্থিতিতে চাপের মধ্যে সমাধান করতে হয়, খরচও বাড়ে। Sprint এখন BDT 55,000 — অডিটের পরে জরুরি সমাধানের খরচ অনেক বেশি হতে পারে।
>
> যদি এখন এগোতে না চান, Gap Report এবং Quotation রেখে যাচ্ছি — অডিটের পরে দরকার হলে সাথে সাথে শুরু করতে পারব।"

## Section 12 — Daily Execution Checklist

Run this every working day.

### Morning (30 minutes)

- [ ] Open tracking sheet (see Section 13)
- [ ] Check overnight WhatsApp replies — respond to all within 30 minutes
- [ ] Check `/gap-scan` submissions from previous day — follow up within 2 hours of seeing them
- [ ] Identify today's 20 new cold contacts from your list
- [ ] Pull today's two follow-up leads from the 48-hour queue

### Active outreach block (2 hours minimum)

- [ ] Send 10 WhatsApp cold openers (Template 3A or 3B)
- [ ] Send 5 LinkedIn connection requests (Template 5A)
- [ ] Make 5 follow-up calls (to leads that have not replied after 48 hours)
- [ ] Push 3 warm leads to the `/gap-scan` form (Template 8A or 8B)

### Afternoon (30 minutes)

- [ ] Review any new `/gap-scan` completions — send acknowledgement within 2 hours
- [ ] Follow up any High/Critical result leads with Sprint pitch call
- [ ] Send quotations to any leads that expressed interest today
- [ ] Update tracking sheet — move leads to correct stage

### End of day (15 minutes)

- [ ] Count: cold contacts sent today, warm follow-ups, `/gap-scan` pushes, Sprint proposals sent
- [ ] Set 48-hour follow-up reminders for today's interested leads
- [ ] Set 7-day follow-up reminders for today's no-reply cold contacts
- [ ] Note any stop conditions encountered — do not leave these unresolved overnight
