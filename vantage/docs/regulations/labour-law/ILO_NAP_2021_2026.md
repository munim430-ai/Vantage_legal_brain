## 2


National Action Plan (NAP) on the Labour Sector of Bangladesh

## Background

In continuation of the series of legal and administrative reforms supported by practical activities to uphold labour rights and workplace safety in Bangladesh,
the Government has decided to develop the following national plan of action through a consultative process. This also has reference to the outcome of 9th
session  of  the  EU-Bangladesh  Joint  Commission  held  in  October  2019.  The  specific  actions  below  will  be  implemented  with  further  engagement  of  the
tripartite constituents and, where appropriate, with the support of the International Labour Organization (ILO) and other development partners.
