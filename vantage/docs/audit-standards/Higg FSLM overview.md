```
Verification (vFSLM)
```
Verification ensures that you accurately completed
all parts of the Higg FSLM – giving your self-
assessment the credibility and accuracy required to
share it publicly.
Verification of the FSLM self-assessment is
conducted by a third- or second-party SLCP-
approved verifier. The verifier is selected and hired
to review a facility site. The verifier is the only
person responsible for validating the accuracy of the
assessment.

Introduction
Verification Type
Onsite Verification: Verifier is required to visit the facility
premises. If a facility wishes to publicly communicate Higg Index
scoring they must complete onsite verification.
Offsite Verification: Verifier is able to conduct the verification
without going to the site, usually by other means such as e-mail,
pictures, web-conference, etc.
```
https://slcp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360030860194-Facility-Guide-to-Virtual-Onsite-Verification-for-v1-4
```
Benefits of Verification
The Verifier has been vetted to ensure they have proper experience and
knowledge.
The Verifier has received in-depth SLCP verification process, Higg
platform training and passed a final exam and are therefore well-
qualified to ensure questions are accurately answered and comparable
from one facility to another.
Official Verified results are captured on Higg.org and can be:
● Shared with business partners in lieu of having another verification
conducted, therefore reducing or eliminating “audit fatigue”
● Tracked, though time, demonstrating year-over-year improvement

FSLM Platform Features | FSLM Scoring
FSLM Scoring
The FSLM scoring system is designed to be universal and drive
improved social performance in manufacturing facilities around
the world. Points are only assigned to universal questions that
drive improved actions, decisions, and practices that lead to better
social and labor sustainability outcomes.
● Total Scores
● Section Scores
● Step Scores
● Risk Scores

FSLM Platform Features | FSLM Cross Host CSV Report
FSLM Cross Host CSV Report
On the Higg platform, brand/retail and large manufacturer users can download
a CSV with an overview of their facilities’ SLCP assessment status.
The CSV indicates whether a facility has completed the SLCP assessment on the
Higg platform or through another accredited host. This feature provides Higg
```
users an overview of detailed reference data (metadata) of all SLCP
```
assessments stored in SLCP Gateway, regardless of whether they are hosted on
the Higg platform or another accredited host, giving them insight into their
facilities’ assessment of social and labor performance.

• New questions in the Data Collection Tool to

• The automation of the Verification Selection to
improve data quality.

make any changes to mapping or scoring.
