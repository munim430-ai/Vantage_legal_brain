## 1. BLA 2026 Compliance Sprint

### What it is
A 3–5 working day intensive that takes a factory from unknown compliance status to a complete, actionable gap report and corrective action plan — delivered in both Bangla and English.

### Who buys it
Factories with an upcoming BSCI/WRAP/SA8000/Sedex audit (4–8 weeks away), or factories that have just received a post-audit CAP from an audit body.

### Price
**BDT 55,000 — all inclusive.**
Payment: 50% before Sprint start (BDT 27,500), 50% on delivery (BDT 27,500).
No hidden fees. No travel charges within Dhaka / Gazipur. Outside these areas: transport actuals billed separately.

### What the client gets
1. Pre-Sprint gap scan (virtual or on-site, max 90 minutes)
2. Written Gap Report — section-by-section BLA 2006 compliance status with evidence requirements
3. Corrective Action Plan (CAP) — prioritized by audit risk (Critical / High / Medium / Low)
4. Policy drafts or redrafts for up to 5 identified gap areas (e.g., leave register, fire drill record, appointment letter template)
5. Priority roadmap — 30-day implementation timeline
6. One 60-minute review call after delivery (within 7 days)

All deliverables in PDF and editable DOCX format.
Bangla summary of all findings for factory HR staff.
English version of all findings for buyer submission.

### What is NOT included
- Representation at any audit
- Legal advice
- Implementation support beyond the 5 policy drafts
- Factory visits outside Dhaka / Gazipur (transport charged separately)

### Upsell path
Sprint → Retainer: pitch at the review call. Frame as "ongoing protection between audits."

## 2. Ongoing Compliance Retainer

### What it is
Monthly retained advisory service. VANTAGE monitors regulatory changes, answers compliance questions, reviews new documents, and keeps the factory's compliance posture current.

### Price
**BDT 30,000/month** — minimum 3-month commitment.
First month billed upfront with the Sprint retainer conversion conversation.

### What the client gets (monthly)
- Regulatory update briefing (when changes occur)
- Up to 3 policy document reviews per month
- WhatsApp access to Munim for compliance questions (response within 24 hours, Mon–Fri)
- Monthly 30-minute check-in call
- Priority booking for any additional Sprint work

### Upsell path
Retainer → Worker Voice: at Month 2 check-in if factory has 500+ workers.

## 3. Worker Voice (Grievance Bot)

### What it is
A WhatsApp-based anonymous grievance intake system for factory workers. Workers send a message to a dedicated number, the bot logs it securely, and factory management receives a weekly aggregated report (no individual worker identified without consent).

### Price
**BDT 15,000/month** — includes setup, hosting, weekly reports, and monthly review.
Setup fee (one-time): **BDT 10,000** (waived for Retainer clients).

### Who it is for
- Factories with 500+ workers
- Factories supplying to buyers requiring worker voice mechanisms (Sedex/SMETA 4-Pillar, Higg)
- Factories that want to identify issues before an auditor does

### What the client gets
- Dedicated WhatsApp Business number for worker grievances
- Bangla-language intake flow (automated)
- Secure logging in Supabase (encrypted, access-controlled)
- Weekly aggregated report to management (no individual worker named without consent)
- Monthly summary for buyer submission
- Worker onboarding poster (A3 print-ready, Bangla)

### Legal note
Worker Voice is deployed under a Worker Voice Agreement signed by the Factory Director. VANTAGE is the data processor; the factory is the data controller. Individual grievances are never shared with management unless the worker consents or there is an imminent safety risk.

### Price
**BDT 8,000/month** — or included free for Retainer clients.

### Who buys it

- Executive summary in Bangla (1 page)
- Sections: Regulatory Changes | Audit Standard Updates | Buyer ESG Movements | VANTAGE Case Note (anonymized)

## 7. Free Gap Scan (Lead Generation Tool)

### What it is
A 90-minute structured assessment — virtual or on-site — that shows a factory exactly which BLA 2006 sections they are non-compliant with, scored by audit risk level. Delivered as a 1-page summary.

### Price
**Free.** No payment, no commitment.

### Purpose
Convert to BLA 2026 Sprint. The Gap Scan gives the factory enough information to understand their risk, but not enough to self-remediate. The Sprint delivers the remediation.

### Conversion script (end of Gap Scan)
> "You have [X] Critical gaps and [Y] High-priority gaps. If an audit happens in the next 60 days, these are the ones that will cause a CAP or a suspension. The Sprint fixes all of them in 5 days for BDT 55,000. Want me to block time for next week?"

### How delivered
- Virtual: shared screen with Google Form-based checklist; summary emailed same day
- On-site: printed checklist (A4, Bangla); summary emailed within 24 hours

## 9. Pricing Rules

- All prices are in BDT only. Never quote in USD to factory clients.
- Western buyer ESG teams: quote in USD at prevailing rate (USD 1 = BDT 110 floor, update quarterly).
- Prices are fixed. No discounts except: (a) Retainer clients on Tech-Pack Matcher; (b) BGMEA workshop attendees in Month 1 only (10% discount on Sprint as launch offer, max 3 clients).
- Never negotiate down from BDT 55,000 on the Sprint. If a factory says it is too expensive, offer the Document Factory (individual documents) instead.
- Payment method: bKash Business / Nagad Business / bank transfer. No cash.
