## 1. What VANTAGE Is

VANTAGE is a compliance technology and advisory service that helps Bangladesh Ready-Made Garment (RMG) factories pass international labour audits, fix BLA 2006 gaps, and manage ongoing compliance — using AI-assisted tools that a solo operator can run profitably.

VANTAGE is **not** a law firm. It does not give legal advice. It provides compliance guidance based on published regulations, audit standards, and operational experience.

## 3. Market

### Geography
Bangladesh. Factories primarily in: Dhaka, Gazipur, Ashulia, Narayanganj, Chittagong EPZ, Comilla EPZ.

### Industry
Ready-Made Garments (RMG) — the single largest export sector in Bangladesh.
- 4,400+ Tier 1 export-oriented factories
- Employs ~4.2 million workers
- Supplies to H&M, Zara, Next, M&S, Primark, Gap, Walmart, PVH, VF Corp

### Regulatory pressure driving demand
- **BLA 2006** (Bangladesh Labour Act) — core statute
- **BLA Amendment 2013** — post-Rana Plaza reforms
- **BLA Amendment 2018** — strengthened provisions
- **BEPZA Regulations** — Export Processing Zone factories
- **BNBC** (Bangladesh National Building Code) — fire/structural safety
- **International audit standards** — BSCI, WRAP, SA8000, Sedex/SMETA, C-TPAT, Higg FEM
- **Western buyer ESG requirements** — EU CSDD Directive, UK Modern Slavery Act

## 4. Target Customers

### Primary Buyer (decision-maker)
- **HR Manager / Head of Compliance** at a Tier 1 RMG factory
- Factory Directors and Managing Directors at smaller factories
- BGMEA member factories (active membership required)

### Trigger Events (when they buy)
1. Upcoming BSCI/WRAP/SA8000/Sedex audit (4–8 weeks away)
2. Failed audit — issued a Corrective Action Plan (CAP)
3. Buyer demand letter citing BLA compliance gaps
4. BLA amendment (regulatory change requiring policy updates)
5. New factory setup requiring compliant HR/labour systems

### Who Does NOT Buy
- Factories with in-house legal/compliance teams of 3+ people
- Factories under government investigation (liability risk)
- Sub-contracted factories without direct buyer relationships

## 5. The Problem VANTAGE Solves

Bangladesh factories know they have BLA/audit gaps. They do not know how to fix them fast, affordably, and in a format that satisfies international buyers. Local lawyers are expensive and don't understand audit frameworks. International consultants are priced in USD. Factory HR managers lack the time and domain knowledge to self-remediate.

VANTAGE fills the gap: **fast, affordable, Bangladesh-specific compliance remediation, delivered in Bangla and English, supported by AI tooling that lets one person serve many clients.**

## 7. Operating Model

Solo operator. One person (Munim) runs everything. All delivery is systematized so it can be completed in under 20 hours per Sprint client. AI agents handle document generation, corpus search, and gap analysis. Munim handles client relationship, final review, and delivery.

**Capacity target:** 6 Sprint clients/month without additional staff.
**Scale trigger:** Hire one delivery associate when reaching 10 active clients/month.

## 8. Channels (Priority Order)

1. **Audit firm referrals** — SGS Bangladesh, Bureau Veritas Bangladesh, Intertek Bangladesh. Referral fee: BDT 3,000–5,000. These leads close at 80%+ because the factory has already been told they need help.
2. **BGMEA/BKMEA workshops** — free 2-hour "BLA 2026: Practical Steps" workshop. One event = 30–80 HR manager contacts. Offer Free Gap Scan at the venue.
3. **WhatsApp broadcast** — 200-contact list from BGMEA member directories. Bangla-language message. 40–60% reply rate. Send BLA 2026 checklist, follow up in 3 days.
4. **LinkedIn** — daily posts targeting HR Managers, Compliance Officers, Western brand ESG teams. 50 new connections/week.
5. **RMG compliance WhatsApp groups** — share Intelligence Brief excerpts, offer Free Gap Scan.
6. **Field visits** — secondary channel only. Free Gap Scan card in hand.
