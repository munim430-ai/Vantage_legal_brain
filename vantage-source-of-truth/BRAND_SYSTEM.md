## 2. Tone of Voice

### Personality
- **Authoritative, not arrogant.** We know Bangladesh labour law. We don't lecture.
- **Direct, not curt.** Factories are busy. Get to the point.
- **Bilingual by default.** Bangla for factory HR staff. English for buyer-facing documents and LinkedIn.
- **Practical, not theoretical.** Every output includes a specific action, not a general recommendation.
- **Trusted partner, not auditor.** We are on the factory's side. We help them fix gaps before the auditor arrives.

### Writing Rules
- Write in second person: "Your factory", "You will receive", "We deliver".
- Use short sentences. Maximum 20 words per sentence in client-facing copy.
- Lead with the outcome, not the process. ("You get a complete CAP in 5 days" not "Our process involves...").
- Prices always in BDT with commas: BDT 55,000 — never "55k" or "₳55,000".
- Dates in Bangladesh format: 30 May 2026, not May 30 or 30/05/26.
- Use "factory" not "plant", "facility", or "establishment" in general copy.
- Use "workers" not "employees" or "labour" in all client copy.
- Use "gap scan" not "audit" when referring to VANTAGE's own assessment (to distinguish from third-party audits).

## 4. Forbidden Phrases

These phrases must never appear in any VANTAGE output — documents, website, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or AI-generated content.

| Forbidden phrase | Why |
|-----------------|-----|
| "legal advice" | VANTAGE is not a law firm. Using this creates legal liability. |
| "we are lawyers" / "our lawyers" | Same reason — VANTAGE has no lawyers on staff. |
| "law firm" | Incorrect entity type. |
| "guarantee compliance" | Cannot guarantee a regulatory outcome. |
| "guarantee you will pass" | Cannot guarantee audit results. |
| "100% compliant" | No factory is ever 100% compliant; this is misleading. |
| "we are approved by BGMEA" | Not approved — only a workshop partner (and only when confirmed). |
| "government approved" | VANTAGE has no government endorsement. |
| "certify your factory" | VANTAGE does not issue certifications. |
| "VANTAGE Legal Brain" | Internal repo name — must never appear in client-facing output. |
| "AI will fix your compliance" | Overstates AI capability. Always: "AI-assisted, specialist-reviewed". |
| "cheap" | Use "affordable" or "cost-effective". |
| "cheap alternative to lawyers" | Positioning risk — implies lower quality. |
| Prices in USD | All client pricing is in BDT only. |
| "guaranteed audit pass rate" | Same as guarantee prohibition above. |

## 6. Document Formatting Standards

All VANTAGE-issued documents (proposals, MSA, Sprint reports, Intelligence Briefs) must follow:

- **Header:** VANTAGE logo (left) | "Keystone Consultancy trading as VANTAGE" (right)
- **Footer:** Page number | govantage.vercel.app | Document version and date
- **Language:** English primary; Bangla translation of key sections where applicable
- **Section numbering:** 1., 1.1, 1.1.1 format
- **Table headers:** Bold, background highlight (primary color at 15% opacity)
- **Regulatory references:** Always cite the full act name and section: "Bangladesh Labour Act 2006, Section 108(1)"
- **Dates:** Full format — "30 May 2026"
- **Currency:** BDT with commas — "BDT 55,000"
- **File naming:** `VANTAGE_[DocumentType]_[FactoryName]_[YYYYMMDD].pdf`

## 7. Social Media Voice

### LinkedIn (English — targeting HR managers, compliance officers, Western buyers)
- Post length: 150–300 words
- Format: 1–3 line hook → context → 3-point list → call to action
- Call to action: "DM me for the free BLA 2026 checklist" or "Comment SCAN for a free gap scan"
- Never post client factory names without written permission
- Weekly cadence: minimum 3 posts/week

### WhatsApp (Bangla — targeting factory HR managers directly)
- Broadcast message: maximum 3 paragraphs
- Must include: specific problem, specific offer, specific next step with a contact number or link
- Never send a broadcast more than once per 2 weeks to the same list
- Always offer to remove from list: "বাদ দিতে চাইলে 'না' লিখুন"

### WhatsApp Group (RMG Compliance Emergency Network)
- Group purpose: share regulatory alerts, not sell
- Maximum 1 promotional message per week in the group
- Share Intelligence Brief excerpts freely to build authority
